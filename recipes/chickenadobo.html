Chicken Adobo

Back to home

Description

Chicken Adobo is the unofficial national dish of the Philippines, known for its tangy, garlicky, and umami-rich flavor.

Simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves, itâ€™s a comforting one-pot meal that pairs beautifully with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs/drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and sugar in a pot.
  2. Add chicken and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 40 minutes.
  4. Remove chicken, brown in a separate pan, then return to sauce.
  5. Simmer until sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Serve with steamed rice.
