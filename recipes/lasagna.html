Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty, comforting dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it combines Italian tradition with home-cooked warmth.

Each bite delivers a satisfying blend of savory ground beef, tangy tomato sauce, and velvety ricotta, all nestled between sheets of tender pasta. It's a timeless favorite that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara or tomato sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
